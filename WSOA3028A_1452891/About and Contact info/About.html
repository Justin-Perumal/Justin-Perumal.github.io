About me!

Hey! My name is Jake and welcome to my blogsite! If youre here it means you might want to know a bit more about me. Well to start off with let me tell you some of the basics. I consider myself to be quite friendly and outgoing... maybe a bit too much initially (^^") I love cooking and the occasional meme, combine the two and we will get along just fine :D

I mentioned I love cooking well thats most likely because I am a profesionally trained chef who is currently working in Johannesburg, South Africa. I have been cooking almost all my life so it was a no brainer. Eventually I would like to open my own restaurant and hey, if you come to my future establishment and show this blog. Being a chef is all good but I do have some background in Computer Science as I studied a bit of it in university. I hope you enjoy what I have to offer and look forward to some engaging content from cooking gadgets to my thoughts on the web and development! Seeya later :D
