Not only did it change the way we perceive and send information, but things like fashion and urban planning had to change as well to accommodate this new medium.

With the internet and the world wide web, we have access to not only a vast array of information, but also to one another. However, the same ideas and opinions can be formed much quicker through the internet due to its nature and how we perceive the information shown to us, further supporting the idea that the medium is more important.

With an advancement of technology, the medium creates a drastic change in the ideas we form from the information provided. The message provides information for how we interact with our surroundings, while the medium changes the scale of that interaction.
